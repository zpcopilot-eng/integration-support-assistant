## Overview

Zalopay provides "a financial technology platform that helps expand your business, increase automation, and optimize your finances." Key offerings include:

- **Cash flow management** through multiple payment and fund management options
- **Automated disbursements** enabling swift, secure fund transfers to customers and partners
- **Operational support** via Merchant Portal and auxiliary services

The platform serves merchants across all sizes seeking to enhance operational and financial efficiency.

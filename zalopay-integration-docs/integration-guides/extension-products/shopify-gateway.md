# Zalopay Gateway Integration for Shopify

Nguồn: https://docs.zalopay.vn/docs/guides/extension-products/shopify/gateway

## Overview

Zalopay offers e-commerce integration for Shopify stores, enabling merchants to accept payments through Zalopay's platform. The service provides access to millions of users and includes features for order management and promotional opportunities.

Key benefits mentioned include:
- Expanded customer reach through Zalopay's user base
- Simplified order management and cost optimization
- Regular promotional opportunities for partners
- Improved payment conversion rates

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Partner Registration
Merchants must register as Zalopay partners at their registration portal. Existing account holders should contact their support team at 1900 54 54 36 or op@zalopay.vn for integration details.

### Step 2: App Installation
The integration involves:
1. Installing the Zalopay app from the Shopify marketplace
2. Authorizing the app installation in Shopify
3. Entering merchant credentials (appID and Key) obtained from the Zalopay merchant dashboard

Alternative partner registration is available during the installation process if needed.

### Step 3: Gateway Activation
Once installed, merchants must:
1. Navigate to Shopify Settings → Payments
2. Add Zalopay as a payment method via provider search
3. Activate Zalopay Payment Gateway

After successful activation, Zalopay appears as an available payment option for customers.
